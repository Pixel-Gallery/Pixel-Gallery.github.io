About

Pixel Gallery is a collection of various pixel arts - originally meant for Microsoft Power Apps, but - reusable for any purpose with the expression of pixels by their HEX color values.

The aim of this site is to collect and share various arts without the troublesome way of recreating them pixel by pixel. If you want to share or submit a work, simply fill out the form under the Submit page. There are no limits on how many submissions you can have!

You want to use one of the arts? You are at the right spot! Read my blog post on how to create a pixel display for yourself, or just visit my github repository to download the existing component to be included in your Canvas App!
